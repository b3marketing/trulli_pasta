About us

We use 100% Durum Semolina shipped directly from a mill in Puglia in Southeast Italy. Puglia, the heel of Italy’s boot, is known for whitewashed hill towns and centuries-old farmland surrounded by hundreds of miles of Mediterranean coastline. Also located in Puglia is the town of Alberobello and the Itria Valley, home to the iconic Trulli, stone huts with distinctive conical roofs and the hometown of Domenico Bosco, our chef and co-owner.

We created Trulli Pasta to carry on our family tradition of handmade pasta with 100% Durum Semolina and 100% Eggs, without the aid of Water. The only way we could achieve the same results, on a larger commercial scale, was to drastically reduce our production to enable the Durum Semolina to be hydrated properly, keeping and respecting my mom’s HIGH STANDARDS!
